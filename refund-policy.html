Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. This policy explains when and how you can request a refund.

Eligibility

  • Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase
  • The product must not have been downloaded, activated, or used
  • Sale and promotional items are non-refundable unless required by law

How to Request a Refund

  • Contact our support team with your order details
  • We will review your request and respond within 3 business days
  • Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method

Exceptions

We reserve the right to refuse refunds if abuse or fraud is suspected.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact our support team.

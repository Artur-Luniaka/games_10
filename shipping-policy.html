Shipping Policy

This policy explains how we handle the delivery of your orders.

Delivery Methods

  • All products are delivered digitally via email or user account
  • No physical shipping is required for digital goods

Delivery Time

  • Most orders are delivered instantly after payment confirmation
  • In rare cases, delivery may take up to 24 hours

Order Issues

  • If you do not receive your order, please check your spam folder
  • Contact our support team if you have not received your product within 24 hours

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my purchased games?

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with download instructions. You can also access your games through your account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Can I get a refund for my purchase?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all digital purchases. Contact our support team to initiate a refund request.

Do you offer technical support?

Absolutely! Our technical support team is available to help with any installation or gameplay issues you may encounter.

Are your games compatible with my system?

Each game page includes detailed system requirements. You can also use our system checker tool to verify compatibility before purchase.

Do you ship physical copies?

We primarily offer digital downloads for instant access. However, some collector's editions may include physical items shipped separately.
